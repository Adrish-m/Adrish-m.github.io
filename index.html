Swami Vivekanand

Born: 12 January 1863
Died: 4 July 1902
Childhood Name: Narendranath Datta
Father's name: Vishwanath Datta
Mother's name: Bhubaneswari Devi

	
Swami Vivekanand (Also known as Swamiji), was an Hindu monk and philosopher. He was a famous disciple of Shri Shri Ramakrishna.
He is also well known for the foundation of Ramakrishna Math and Mission , whose headquarter is in Belur Math.
